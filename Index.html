About the Candidate

Name: Sherrie Cohen

Office: Judge, Philadelphia Municipal Court

Party: Democratic Party

Sherrie Cohen is a longtime tenant rights attorney and community advocate. She has worked with neighborhood groups on housing justice, public education and criminal justice reform. Sherrie believes that courts should treat everyone with respect, listen carefully and apply the law fairly.

  • Fair treatment for all people in Municipal Court
  • Access to justice for low-income residents
  • Strong respect for civil and human rights
